Our Impact



















Students from SICSR collaborated with the Adar Poonawalla Clean City Initiative, leading impactful cleanup drives across Pune, contributing to a cleaner and healthier planet.

In partnership, students actively participated in community cleanup initiatives, revitalizing local spaces by removing tons of waste, making parks, streets, and public areas more enjoyable for everyone.

Focusing on reducing plastic pollution, students partnered with the initiative for awareness campaigns and cleanup activities, working towards a sustainable and plastic-free future.

Beyond physical cleanup, SICSR students and the initiative initiated workshops, seminars, and awareness programs to educate and inspire communities about waste management and environmental conservation.

Supported by the Adar Poonawalla Clean City Initiative, cleanup drives have forged strong partnerships with local communities, building a network of environmental stewards committed to keeping surroundings clean and green.

Celebrate the achievements of SICSR students and the Adar Poonawalla Clean City Initiative. Join our ongoing journey to create a positive impact through community-driven environmental initiatives.

Cleanup Drives

River Cleanup

Our dedicated students took part in a impactful river cleanup initiative. Through collaboration with the Adar Poonawalla Clean City Initiative, we made a significant difference in improving the cleanliness of rivers in Pune.

By actively participating in cleanup drives, we aim to raise awareness about environmental conservation and inspire others to join us in our mission for a cleaner and greener world.

Pune Railway Station Cleanup

In collaboration with the Adar Poonawalla Clean City Initiative, Climate Troopers organized a comprehensive cleanup drive at Pune Railway Station. Students from the Symbiosis Institute of Computer Studies and Research actively participated in this initiative.

The cleanup aimed to create awareness about maintaining cleanliness in public spaces and contribute to the Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. Students collected and properly disposed of waste, emphasizing the importance of environmental responsibility.

The Pune Railway Station Cleanup project showcased the dedication of Climate Troopers in making a positive impact on the local community and fostering a sense of responsibility towards our environment.

Hill Cleanup

Climate Troopers, in collaboration with the Adar Poonawalla Clean City Initiative, undertook a significant hill cleanup project. Our dedicated students worked towards preserving the natural beauty of the hills by actively participating in cleanup drives and environmental conservation efforts.

The Hill Cleanup initiative aims to raise awareness about the impact of waste on natural ecosystems and inspire a sense of responsibility towards maintaining the ecological balance of our hills and surrounding areas.

Sarasbaug Pond Cleanup

Climate Troopers took a significant step in environmental conservation by actively participating in the cleanup of Sarabaug Pond. In collaboration with the Adar Poonawalla Clean City Initiative, our dedicated students worked tirelessly to restore the beauty and health of this vital water body in Pune.

The Sarabaug Pond Cleanup initiative aimed to address issues such as water pollution and habitat degradation. Through community-driven efforts, we strive to create a positive impact on local ecosystems and inspire a sense of responsibility for preserving natural resources.
